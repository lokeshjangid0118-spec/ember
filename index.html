Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your approach "architectural"? +

We design weddings as temporary monuments, creating sculptural installations and structural elements that transform spaces into architectural masterpieces for your celebration.

Do you work internationally? +

Yes, we create architectural weddings worldwide. Our team travels globally to design bespoke celebrations in any location that inspires your vision.

What's included in your services? +

Complete architectural wedding design including structural installations, couture floral architecture, cinematic lighting, custom fabrication, and full event production management.

How far in advance should we book? +

We recommend booking 12-18 months in advance for architectural weddings, as our bespoke installations require extensive design, fabrication, and coordination time.

What's your design process like? +

We begin with an architectural consultation, create detailed 3D renderings, develop custom fabrication plans, and provide full project management from concept to celebration day.
